Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our restaurant profitability software.

Is this software right for small and mid-sized restaurant owners?

Yes — Pricing Assistant was built specifically for SMB restaurant owners who need accurate profitability calculations without expensive enterprise software. It runs on Mac and Windows with a one-time purchase. No subscription required.

How does Pricing Assistant improve restaurant profitability?

It calculates your true food cost, labor cost, and monthly overhead per menu item so you always know your exact profit margin before setting a price. No more guessing or losing money on underpriced dishes.

What's the difference between Standard and Pro?

Standard covers up to 8 ingredients, labor, overhead, and gives you a recommended price with margin check — perfect for pricing individual items. Pro adds unlimited ingredients, competitor price analysis, a what-if scenario planner, full menu manager with CSV export, and advanced labor tiers. Ideal when managing a full menu.

Is there a monthly subscription?

No. Pricing Assistant is a one-time purchase — $49.99 for Standard or $89.99 for Pro. You own the software outright with no recurring fees.

Does it work on Mac and Windows?

Yes. After purchase, you choose Mac or Windows on the download page. Both versions are fully supported.
