Cookie Policy

Last updated July 20, 2026

This policy explains how CR8 Autos uses cookies and similar browser-storage technologies on this website.

What these technologies are

Cookies are small text files saved by a website. Local storage is a similar browser feature that remembers information on your device. They can support core features, remember preferences, and help a site operate securely.

Storage we use

  • Essential and preference storage: used to remember your cookie choice and support website features.
  • Form and security technologies: service providers involved in appointment or photo submissions may use technical storage required to process and secure requests.
  • Third-party resources: fonts, icons, maps, hosting, or linked services may receive technical request information and may apply their own storage when you interact with them.

Your choices

Select “Accept” or “Essential only” in the notice. You can reopen the notice at any time with Cookie Settings in the footer. You may also delete or block storage in your browser. Blocking essential storage may prevent preferences or certain website features from working as intended.

Current optional tracking

CR8 Autos does not currently activate advertising cookies through this consent tool. If optional analytics or advertising technologies are added later, this policy and the consent controls should be updated before those technologies are enabled.

Changes

We may update this policy when website technologies or service providers change. The revision date above identifies the current version.
